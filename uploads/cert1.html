Keys to Success

  • Stays actively current of industry trends and directions;
  • Demonstrates all benefits of energy efficiency;
  • Possesses technical competencies to gather and assess appropriate information;
  • Possesses foundational competencies in engineering;
  • Applies analytical decision-making process to include both financial goals and energy saving goals;
  • Develops practical solutions beyond energy consumption.
  • Ensures that all key stakeholders are properly informed.
  • Applies systems thinking when identifying appropriate solutions and/or troubleshooting.
  • Informs and educates stakeholders as a means to change behavior.
  • Effectively influences others to achieve goals.
